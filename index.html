This is Christina's webside about world wide polution

Plastic pollution is one of the major issues on the planet. Air, land and aquatic pollution. The life under the water is in danger both due to overfishing and to the rise of pollution in the oceans. The consumption of goods, as a result of the population growth, has consequently increased the pollutants. Whereas, by 2050, it is estimated that rubbish will exceed the number of aquatic organisms.
